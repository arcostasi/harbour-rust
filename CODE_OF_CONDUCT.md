## Our Standard

We want Harbour Rust to be a technically serious and welcoming project for contributors from different backgrounds, countries, and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- communicate with respect and precision;
- critique ideas without attacking people;
- be generous with context, sources, and review feedback;
- help keep the project accessible to new contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or hostile personal attacks;
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior;
- deliberate plagiarism or knowingly submitting material without proper rights;
- persistent disruption of technical collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, decline contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or the people working in it.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct concerns to the maintainers through the repository contact channels available at the time of reporting.
