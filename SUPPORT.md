## Asking for Help

Good support requests usually include:

- the fixture or source file involved;
- the command that was run;
- expected behavior;
- observed behavior;
- version or commit reference.

Questions may be asked in English or Portuguese (Brazil). English remains the canonical language for documentation and final policy text.

## What Support Means Here

This is a community project maintained in available time. Support is best-effort, public, and collaborative.
